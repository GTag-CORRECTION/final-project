History of Badminton

From ancient games to Olympic sport

Birth of Modern Badminton

Modern badminton was developed in England during the 1870s. British army officers returning from India brought the game of Poona back home. The sport became popular at Badminton House, the estate of the Duke of Beaufort, which is where the name "Badminton" originated.

As the sport grew, official rules were created and organized competitions began to appear throughout England.

Major Milestones

1873

Badminton introduced in England.

1893

Badminton Association of England founded.

1934

International Badminton Federation established.

1992

Badminton becomes an Olympic sport.

Today

Played by millions across the globe.

Modern Badminton

Today, badminton is a global sport played at professional and recreational levels. Countries like China, Indonesia, Denmark, and India dominate international competition.
